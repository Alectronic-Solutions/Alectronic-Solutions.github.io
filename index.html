Common Questions

FAQ

How long does a website take to build?

Our "Landing Page" package is delivered in 1 week. Custom "Professional" sites typically take 3-4 weeks depending on the complexity and content readiness.

Do you offer payment plans?

Yes, for projects over $2,000, we typically split payments into 50% upfront and 50% upon completion. We can also discuss milestone-based payments for enterprise projects.

Will I be able to edit the website myself?

Absolutely. Our "Professional" package includes a Content Management System (CMS) that allows you to easily edit text, images, and blog posts without touching code.

Does the price include hosting?

The price covers design and development. We can assist you in setting up hosting (approx. $10-20/month) or host it for you as part of a maintenance package.

The Real ROI of Professional Web Design

First Impressions Matter. It takes about 50 milliseconds (thats 0.05 seconds) for users to form an opinion about your website that determines whether theyll stay or leave. Visual appeal plays a significant role in this first impression.

The Numbers Don't Lie

A professional website isn't just an expense; it's an investment. Studies show that a well-designed UI can raise your websites conversion rate by up to 200%, and a better UX design could yield conversion rates up to 400%.

Key Benefits of Custom Design:

  • Credibility: 75% of consumers admit to making judgments on a company's credibility based on the company's website design.
  • Speed: Custom code is lighter than template builders, leading to faster load times and better SEO rankings.
  • Identity: Stand out from competitors who use generic templates.

Investing in good design is investing in the longevity of your brand.

Why API Security Matters

APIs are the backbone of modern web applications, connecting your data to the frontend. However, they are also a major target for attackers. In 2024, API attacks increased by 400%.

Our Security Standards

At Alectronic Solutions, we don't take shortcuts. Every backend we build includes:

  • JWT Authentication: Secure, stateless user sessions.
  • Rate Limiting: Preventing DDOS and brute force attacks.
  • Input Sanitization: Preventing SQL injection and XSS attacks.
  • HTTPS/TLS: Encryption in transit for all data.
